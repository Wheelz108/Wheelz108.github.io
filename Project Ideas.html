I'm actually in the middle of developing a game at the moment. I've got a small team consisting of me, two other programmers, a writer and an artist. It's past the first papaer prototyping stage and I'm in the middle of developing the first prototype in C-Sharp's console interface.

To give a brief summary it's a turn-based RPG, you and your enemy have a team of six party members with three on the field at a time. Each action you perform adds to a 'point' counter and at the end of the round each side's points are tallyed up and the side with the least points goes first next round.

Right now the only actions each person can perform is an attack action and a unique action specific to that character, some of these include healing party members, halving damage taken, having a chance to dodge an attack etc.

To be honest it's rather complex, but I'm almost finished this prototype, so we'll see where the idea goes from there.
